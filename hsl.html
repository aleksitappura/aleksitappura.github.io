Helsinki Region Transport

Designing for accessibility

Background

Helsinki Region Transport is in charge of the transport system planning and regional public transport in Greater Helsinki. Some 370 million journeys are made on HSL's transport services annually.

My role

  • Research & Concepts
  • UI/UX Design
  • Prototyping

Designing for accessibility

Because HSL is a public service provider, a large part of our work has been ensuring that digital products that we build are accessible, and follow the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG 2.1), as required by EU Accessibility Directive, starting from September 2019.

Designing for such a diverse user base might be challenging at times, but accessibility benefits everyone, and contributes to overall usability. Personally, this experience has been eye-opener for me, and influences the way I work as a designer.

Note: Due to the fact that most of the work I've done at HSL is still unreleased, I cannot share visuals or specifics at this time. I'm happy to discuss more about design challenges and learnings in person, so don't hesitate to get in touch via email.
