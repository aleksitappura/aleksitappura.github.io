Whim has raised over 21 million euros to build a service, that combines taxis, public transport, car services and bike sharing into one seamless experience.

While working at the design studio Kallan & Co, we designed the award winning user interface and user experience for the Whim mobile app.

City bikes integration

We started designing the city bikes integration for the Whim app by first researching and testing the local city bike system in Helsinki. Insights we gathered in the field helped us to create a digital user interface that was both intuitive and easy to replicate for other micro-mobility services like electric scooters.
